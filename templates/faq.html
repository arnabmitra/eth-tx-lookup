Frequently Asked Questions

What is GEX?

GEX (Gamma Exposure) measures the rate of change in delta. It helps predict price movements based on options positioning.

How do I use this tool?

Enter a stock symbol and expiration date to see the GEX chart. Green bars indicate support levels, red bars indicate potential volatility.

What does positive GEX mean?

Positive GEX acts as a stabilizer, creating support/resistance levels where dealers buy dips and sell rips.

What does negative GEX mean?

Negative GEX acts as an accelerator, amplifying price movements as dealers chase the trend.
